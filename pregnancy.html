My ideal pregnancy app:

1. Relieve the anxiety of the expactant parents.
Expectant parents always want the best care for pregnant women and their babies, but when they get pregnant for the first time, they often lack knowledge. More importantly, when they find something that doesn't fit their imagination and don't know how to deal with it, their stress will increase. I hope that my app can clearly tell them what their daily tasks are and help them get through this transition period healthily.

2. Promote scientific knowledge, not interference
For the current pregnancy applications, a common pain point is information overload. Each app provides a large number of reports and articles, and many anxious parents post some anxious topics. It's too unfriendly for pregnant women. I hope to create a convenient, healthy, scientific and positive atmosphere for them to effectively absorb information and maintain a good body condition.

3. Convenient tool to record body changes
Perhaps the most interesting thing about pregnancy is to keep track of your body changes and write down a diary. It will be an important memory for the future. This app will be the most convenient and effective recording tool for expectant parents.

My ideal operation model:

This is the model that shows who and how to solve the problems. In this way, parents can get guidance by App to save the high cost of face-to-face private education. Researchers in related fields can also concentrate on research and gain profits by achieving economies of scale.
