Mobirise

Exhibition App

Time:  2019.1
Theme:  Mobile design
Team:  Individual work
Role:  UX/UI Designer

Context and Problem:
With the improvement of living standards in China, people's spiritual needs are gradually increasing, especially the pursuit of art. Statistics show that more and more people are willing to visit museums, art galleries or other exhibitions. However, the services of these exhibitions do not really keep pace with the visitor growth. The project is to improve the experience of visiting exhibitions by App designing.
