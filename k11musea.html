Stage 1 /  Discover


1. The Current NDP Services:

a. Pre-booked guided tour:  Target kindergarten to lower primary students and families. 90 minutes with show and tell about the stories of arts and species in the shopping mall and the small park. HKD $200/Person, half price for kids below 18.
b. App: Provide several games related to the theme of the park, like "find the fish", "draw a flower" and some other sharing features to strengthen the experience of the guided tour. Free.
c. Experiential workshops: Farming, DIY...seasonal activites or brand cooperation. HKD $300~$1000/Person.
d. Venue rental: With the elegant environment, it's available to rented for dinner, afternoon tea, cocktail, wedding and VIP events. Mainly B2B.

After the analysis of the DNP's business model and some background research, we have some initial understanding of the core values of NDP:

Previous Next
  1. Urban farming - or community gardening. Under the premise of the shortage of land resources in Hong Kong, it is very difficult to find places with real soil in the urban area. It provide a place for urban people to touch and feel nature in the noisy city.
  2. Nature education - as its main services, the guided tour, are targeted to young kids and families, nature education must be one of NDP's vision. It's a collection of diverse species, also a good opportunity for kids to learn something.
  3. Eco art - K11 series pioneered the museum-retail concept based on its connection of art, people and nature, and NDP is no exception. There are sculptures, artifacts, even music that are closely combined with the nature theme, which is unique in the industry.

2. Initial Observation:

Here is the record of our first NDP visit and we did a quick summary of our observations:

We separated our analysis into five categories to compare, which are, position/hardware, service/product, price, channels of booking, and promotion platforms.

Conclusion: Confirm the market positioning of NDP by SWOT/TOWS

2. Quantitative Research: Paid Nature-Related Activity Survey (questionnaire)

In quantitative research, we hope to collect quantifiable data and conduct systematic research to analyze the habits of protential customers. Thus we created a questionnaire to investigate on people's habit of attending Paid Nature-Related activities which are events with the theme of nature while people are paying to attend. And here are the part of results of the collected response and analysis:

We distributed the questionnaire both online and offline and targeting to the age group ranged from 18-50 years old. The online participants are random (mainly millennials), the offline participants are visitors of K11 Musea (mainly millennials or families with kids ).

For the types of entertainment activities that they usually participate in. The data showed people are all more inclined to leisure activities. Leisure activities are characterized by spending money and various types, so we can infer that people willing to pay for meaningful, fresh and interesting leisure activities.

When asked about paying for nature related activities, only 34% of people had participated in such activities, and 48% had only heard about them. For the information channel, we can see that social media and recommendation from friends are the most effective channels for information transmission. 

We also asked about the interviewees‚Äô opinions on several nature related activities that are common in the present market. The results show that no matter it is the tour or the workshop, people prefer to do the outdoor activities. 

The graphs show that people are more willing to participate in nature related activities with friends and family, and they are mostly likely concerning about the health benefits and the interaction with friends and family.

In terms of suggestions for improvement of nature related activities, we found that people are very interested in gaining knowledge and skills related to nature, and they also keen on the high-tech experience of nature.

3. Final solution:

Before talking about our final solution, we‚Äôd like to address the value, vision and goals that our new service wants to provide to customers:

Value
To explore the connection between Nature and Human beings by discovering, digging, collecting and digesting. We want visitors to take part in the nature experience process with physical involvements.

Vision
We are committed to create a system to help NDP utilizing current and potential resources for constructing an experience-oriented platform welcoming all generations to join and celebrate the beauty of urban bio-diversity.

Goals
‚Ä¢ To promote the understanding of how human can contribute to bio-diversified natural environment, and what nature can provide to us in return.
‚Ä¢ To demonstrate the sustainable and eco-friendly use of natural resources in an urban setting.

Here we are promoting a new service system to NDP which can be divided into four main parts: game, juice, app and promotion. 

(1) Game

Those who are interested in participating this nature discovering activity, will follow clear signs arriving at the reception on 8th Floor. NDP staff will deliver them the game map after paying for the entrance.

It is an exploration based game, providing a learn-in-play experience, the value of the game is to trigger self-motivated learning by peeking into the hidden knowledge.

With the help of the map, visitors are encouraged to head out to the park and discover all the areas including Earth, Sky, Sea, Art sections. There are guidelines on the map that lead visitors to look for treasures that are located randomly around the whole park.

Inside each of the treasure, there is information of a unique plant or animal that can be found in Hong Kong, with a stamp for capturing the logo onto the map. These logos represent a corresponding element, as visitors can see at the back of the map with the information about relationship between elements and ingredients. By collecting these elements, visitors will then be able to generate different ingredients for their juice later on.

Prototype details:

We hope to design an object related to nature as the key "treasure" in the whole game. This object is supposed to be get after the simple exploration by customers. Besides, we also hope it can carry some educational impact. So we designed the model in the picture, which is similar to a trunk and will be hidden in the different point of the park. When the customer finds it, they can use it to stamp the corresponding element in the map to prove that the element is collected. At the same time, the inside of this model cntain a brief introduction of the natural elements, enabling customers to acquire knowledges while seeking for treasures.

(2) Juice

During the staying period, the more elements visitors collect, the more ingredients they can choose to put into their juice.

The ingredients are supposed to be provided not just by the farms inside NDP, but also alternatively can be supplied by other urban farms in HK, that might potentially connect NDP with other business opportunities which share a similar ideology on urban bio-diversity.

About the selection of ingredients for NDP, we recommend to purchase seeds from professional urban planning source, like Urbanfarmer.com. Our recommendation would be to start with: kiwi, blueberry, grape for fruits; carrot, corn, peppers for vegetables, lavender, mint, rosemary for herbs. All these species are capable of being grown in an urban setting, aligning with the vision of NDP of promoting urban bio-diversity.

#Drink Challenge:

When visitors are happy with the choices of ingredients they have, they can then bring the map to the juice bar, NDP staff will then check the availability of the ingredients on the map and visitor can choose any combination of ingredients to mix inside the drink.

The juice may taste nice or bad, it represents the whole NDP experience from memory into a physical object that they can digest both with their stomach and mind. Allowing visitors to realize the cycle of nature and human beings through the game and the reward.

There is a booth space next to the juice bar, where visitors can taste the juice in front of the back drop while being recorded into a filtered video with fun effects. We want to capture the real and funny emotions that visitors express and encourage them to share the video on their social media, tagging NDP and #NDP Drink Challenge.

As the high acceptancy and popularity of healthy drinks in HK‚Äôs drinking culture, we think an online challenge could be the fastest way to spread out the words among all the social media users in HK for both targeted generations.  

(3) App

After the whole physical experience in NDP, we recommend visitors to download a new NDP App for extended experience of nature learning. Ideally, we hope the following app functions can open a cycle of promotional behaviors through digital media.

In the app, they can receive coupon and discounts by collecting more elements. They can also refer friends and family to enjoy the discount for their coming NDP visit. Such arrangement could be intangible materials to trigger recurring visit and draw more new customers. Details of the plants and elements inside treasures can be found on the app, with rich educational materials for self-motivated learner to expand their knowledge about nature.

During visitors recurring visit, they can unlock more ingredients, which are only available on the app, so that next time they have a wider choice of the ingredients inside their new juice.

(4) Promotion

Generally, ‚ÄúPromotion‚Äù has influence on the whole process of the activity, including trend-making social media impact, recurring visit triggering and new customers drawing.

On the one hand, as shown in the image, when tourists taste their own juice, the experience in this park will be transformed into taste, and then generate their own memories. At the same time, if the users are active on social media and are willing to participate in our #Drink challenge, it can help a lot to promote our activities.

On the other hand, when the visitors finish the whole process, if they continue to use our app, they can extend the experience on their mobile phones by unlocking more juice ingredients and getting coupons. We believe that this kind of extended experience can stimulate their desire to visit again and increase the possibility of attracting new users.
